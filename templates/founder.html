Founder

Prof. Achyuta Samanta
KIIT, KISS, KTT
Prof. Achyuta Samanta is the founder of Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT); Kalinga Institute of Social Sciences (KISS), Social Entrepreneur, Philanthropist and a Social Activist. KISS provides free accommodation, food, healthcare, and education from class 1 to post-graduation with vocational training free of cost. He is the founder and a constant supporter of KTT helping it thrive on spirituality, good deeds towards a path of scientific and religious liberty of the state and the nation. His spirit and hard work has given KTT the opportunities to preach the path of righteousness and holiness. His devotion towards the “art of giving” at all the levels of the society inspires social workers and activists throughout the globe to contribute to the society and help others grow. His vision and mission has been to educate, employ, cultivate and serve the society by the simple act of giving back to the humanity.
